ABOUT ME

Hello, I am Federico, Senior Computer Vision & ML Engineer! 💻 😊

Currently, I work as Senior Computer Vision | ML Engineer at Technology Data Reply IT, in the CV Business Unit. Together with my team, I develop innovative solutions for challenging problems of international customers using Deep Learning models and MLOps practises and Data Science techniques. Apart from being a tech enthusiast, I am a passionate Italian telecommunications engineer, with a strong focus on visual data and signal processing. In particular, I've specialised myself about Computer Vision, Image Processing and Deep Learning but I like to have a perspective on the business and innovation side too. I have obtained a EIT Joint MSc in Visual Computing Communication at KTH in Stockholm and University of Trento, on top of a BSc in Information and Communications Engineering at University of Trento. Trust me, I am an electrical engineer! I also had two amazing internships in computer vision startups, ManoMotion in Stockholm, and Xtensa Srl in Trento. In free time I love to watch movies, going to the theatre, draw: this is why I also have a creative and design-inspired mind for visual arts. In this context, as a side work, I personally created and designed the Engel Logo for a Startup Lab Course at Clab in Trento in 2019.
